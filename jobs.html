Job description

We have been retained to find experienced candidates for this Product Manager position located in beautiful New England. The client manufactures Consumer Products sold in the “big box” stores and through local dealers.
The ideal candidate can be an Associate Product Manager or Assistant Product Manager ready for the next step up or a Product Manager looking for a new career opportunity.

    Duties:

  • Carrying out linguistic and semantic research independently and jointly
  • Semantic, linguistic adjustment of products for perfect operation
  • Conduct and manage independent researches
  • Examining and validating language problems in product development
  • Integration of linguistic solutions and ideas into existing platforms

    Requirements:

  • High motivation to discover new semantic methods, Significant research, methodological experience
  • Passion for natural language semantics
  • High degree of information theoretical background and significant mathematical background
  • Independent research work on the basis of target definitions
  • Good command of English Candidates from AI/machine learning/Data field with linguistic and emotion modeling experience (without linguist background ) are also welcomed.

Known languages:

Good command of English
